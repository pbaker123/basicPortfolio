About Me

I am happily married since September 1, 2013. We do not have any children, but we would like to have one or two children in the next five years. My sister-in-law moved to Austin in April of 2017, and she is also looking to have children in the next few years. My wife and I decided it would be great when we have children for them to know their cousins. So, we relocated from Suwanee, Georgia to Austin Texas in August of 2017.

I have played soccer since I was 8 years old. I’ve had to slow down in the last few years due to injuries. In the spare time, I have picked up pilates, yoga, hiking, and sailing. My brother-in-law and I recently purchased a 1984 S2 s69 sailboat. We are currently retrofitting her for a more modern look.

I began my career as a manager at a quick serve restaurant chain called Zaxby’s. This allowed me to pay my way through college, and I graduated with a Bachelor’s degree in Managerial Sciences. At that time, the job market was fairly soft, so I was fortunate to have a friend that needed an additional temporary worker at Toshiba. As soon as a posting for another job became available I applied and moved into a permanent role. I’ve now been with Toshiba for nine years, but the company has morphed and merged several times. Currently, I am working on education for the self-checkout line of products.

I am currently enrolled in a full-stack web-development boot camp. When I graduate, I wish to start a new career in this field.
